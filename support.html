Frequently Asked Questions

What's a GoDex?

GoDex is an online Pokedex checklist for Pokemon GO. Use it to keep track of your Pokemon collection outside of the game.

How does it work?

Just tick off all the Pokemon you have so that you can see all the ones you still need.

What does the "Hide" button do?

It hides the Pokemon that you ticked off so that you only see the ones you still need.

How can I save my selections?

All your changes are automatically saved in your browser!

Can I transfer my collection to another device?

Yes, at the moment you have to press the "Export" button which will copy your unique code, send this code to another device and press the "Import" button to paste it in.
